## What this ships with

This template starts with a calm editorial shell, complete route structure, and
local MDX loading so a later brand pass can focus on real words and images
instead of layout scaffolding.

## Included surfaces

- Home
- About
- Articles list and detail
- Projects list and detail
- Services list and detail
- Generated metadata routes such as `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt`, and share images

## What you should replace first

- Site metadata in `src/lib/site-config.ts`
- Navigation and homepage modules in `src/content/site.ts`
- Sample MDX entries inside `src/content/**`
- Share imagery, icons, and screenshots for your public release

The goal is to keep the public-content pass mostly inside `src/content/` and a
small set of config files rather than spreading edits across every route.
